CAMPUS INFRASTRUCTURE:

The children enjoy a beautifully manicured, Wi-Fi enabled campus, meticulously maintained playing-fields and sporting facilities,
fully-air-conditioned classrooms and conference halls with well-equipped libraries, high-tech laboratoriesand an exclusive dining
experience. Agrim World School boasts of the most advanced infrastructure.



School Building

The Education City, of which the Orchard Academy School is an integral part, covers 4 acres and accommodates with a built-up area of
about 5000 sq. ft. The entire campus is fully air-conditioned with 24/7 power backup. The campus has several playing fields
interspersed with grassy knolls, beautiful gardens and tree-lined avenues. Thoughtfully designed, all facilities in the school
are within close walking distance.

We offer not just classrooms, but workshops for learning:
⇨ Flexible, spacious subject-based classrooms.
⇨All teaching areas are equipped with technological aids: a PC projector, camera and visualiser.
⇨Modular furniture used by the Primary School allows the rapid reorganisation of the classroom to accommodate group work and differentiation.
